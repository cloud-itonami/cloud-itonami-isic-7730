# Governance

cloud-itonami-isic-7730 follows open-source governance with transparent
decision-making documented in ADRs.

Scope: Back-office rental-record logging, post-return maintenance-inspection
scheduling, rental-fleet restock/replacement coordination, equipment-safety-concern
escalation.

Out-of-scope: Directly finalizing an equipment-safety-clearance decision (e.g.
certifying a returned unit as safe to re-rent without inspection), and overriding
an equipment-safety-authority decision.

See README.md for complete CRITICAL scope exclusions.
